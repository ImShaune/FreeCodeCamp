The Sphynx Cat

The Egyptian cat

Breed standards:

  • 1) - Wedge-shaped heads with prominent cheekbones.
  • 2) - Large, lemon-shaped eyes.
  • 3) - Very large ears with hair on inside, but soft down on outside base.
  • 4) - Well-muscled, powerful neck of medium length.
  • 5) - Medium length torso, barrel-chested, and full, round abdomen, sometimes called a pot belly.
  • 6) - Paw pads thicker than other cats, giving the appearance of walking on cushions.
  • 7) - Whiplike, tapering tail from body to tip, (sometimes with fur all over tail or a puff of fur on the tip, like a lion).
  • 8) - Muscular body.
